Biography

Muhammad Kabir received his BS and MS degree in Computer Science from Islamia College Peshawar and Abdul Wali Khan University Mardan Pakistan in 2012 and 2016 respectively. Currently (from Sep 2016-onward), he is a Ph.D. candidate at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanjing University of Science and Technology, Nanjing, China. He is a member of Pattern Recognition and Bioinformatics Group. His current research interests includes Machine Learning and Deep Learning in Bioinformatics and Image Processing.
