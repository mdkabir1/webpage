Biography

Muhammad Kabir, born in 1990, received his Bachelor and Masters degree in Computer Science from Islamia College Peshawar, and Abdul Wali Khan University Mardan Pakistan in 2012 and 2016 respectively. Currently (from September 2016-onward), he is a Ph.D. candidate at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanjing University of Science and Technology (NJUST), Nanjing, Jiangsu Province, P.R. China. He is a member of Pattern Recognition and Bioinformatics Group. His current research interests includes Machine Learning and Deep Learning in Bioinformatics and Image Processing.
